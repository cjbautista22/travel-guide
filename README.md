\## Best Season to Visit



The best time to visit depends on what you’re looking for:



\- Dry season (November to May): Ideal for outdoor activities, sightseeing, and travel.

\- Cool months (December to February): Most comfortable weather with cooler temperatures.

\- Wet season (June to October): Lush scenery and fewer crowds, but expect frequent rain.



Overall, the most recommended time is during the dry and cooler months for the best experience.
