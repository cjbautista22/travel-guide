\## 🍢 Local Snacks



When traveling, trying local snacks is a must! Here are some popular Filipino street foods and treats:



\- Fish Balls – Deep-fried fish paste balls served with sweet or spicy sauce  

\- Kwek-Kwek – Orange-coated quail eggs, crispy on the outside  

\- Banana Cue – Caramelized fried bananas on a stick  

\- Turon – Fried spring roll filled with banana and jackfruit  

\- Taho – Warm tofu with arnibal (sweet syrup) and sago pearls  



These snacks are affordable, delicious, and give you a true taste of local culture.
